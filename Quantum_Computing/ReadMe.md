# Quantum Information Notes & Implementations

This repository documents my transition from classical cryptography to quantum information theory, with a focus on quantum cryptography and foundational concepts.

## Background

I hold an M.Sc. in Cryptography (Applied Mathematics) and have research experience in secure multiparty computation (MPC), including protocols such as SPDZ. My background also includes algorithm design and high-performance programming (Python, Fortran, CUDA).

This project reflects my effort to extend my expertise toward quantum information theory and quantum cryptography.
